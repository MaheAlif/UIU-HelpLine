Most asked questions

How many programs UIU offer for Undergraduate degree?

There are 9 number of Program: BBA, BBA in AIS, BSECO, BSCSE, BSEEE, BSCE, BSSEDS, MSJ, English.

How many trimester in a year?

UIU offers 3 trimesters in a year: Spring, Summer & Fall. In general, these trimesters start in February, June & October respectively.

From where I can collect the admission form?

Admission Form is available at UIU Admission Office on working days between 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM. on payment of Tk. 1000/= (Non Refundable). Also, Admission Form can be purchased and submit via online at admission.uiu.ac.bd
